🗣 每日对话 Day 01 — 周会汇报工作进度
周一 | 第 1 周主题：周会沟通

─────────────────────────
📖【完整对话】
─────────────────────────

> 场景：周一上午的团队周会（standup），5 个人参会。今天轮到 Alex (后端开发) 和 Maria (产品经理) 汇报。

**A — Alex (Backend Developer)**
**B — Maria (PM)**

**A (Alex):** Morning everyone. I'll go first. Last week, I finished the user authentication module, so we're **on track** with the sprint goal.
> 大家早上好。我先来。上周我完成了用户认证模块，所以我们的冲刺目标**进度正常**。

**B (Maria):** That's great, Alex. Just to confirm — does that include the password reset flow?
> 太好了，Alex。确认一下——包含密码重置流程吗？

**A (Alex):** Yes, that's all done and tested. However, I'm a bit **behind schedule** on the API documentation. I was planning to finish it by Friday, but I got **pulled into** a production issue on Thursday.
> 对，全部完成并且测试过了。不过 API 文档有点**落后于进度**了。我原本计划周五完成，但周四被一个线上问题**拉过去了**。

**B (Maria):** I saw that incident in the channel. Is it resolved now?
> 我在频道里看到了那个事故。现在解决了吗？

**A (Alex):** Yeah, it's **wrapped up**. The database connection pool was hitting the limit. I increased the pool size and added monitoring. But the docs will probably slip to **early next week**.
> 对，已经**处理完了**。是数据库连接池达到上限的问题。我增大了连接池并加了监控。但文档可能要拖到**下周初**。

**B (Maria):** Got it. As long as we have the docs ready before the **sprint demo**, that should be fine. Let's **circle back** on Wednesday to check the progress.
> 明白了。只要在**冲刺演示**之前把文档准备好就行。我们周三**再回头确认**一下进度。

**A (Alex):** Sounds good. I'll make sure to **prioritize** that. Oh, one more thing — I need the updated API spec from the frontend team. I've been **blocked on** that since last Tuesday.
> 好的。我会**优先处理**那个。对了还有一件事——我需要前端团队更新的 API 规格文档。从上周二开始就被这个**卡住了**。

**B (Maria):** Ah, I'll **follow up** with them after this meeting. They should have it ready by **EOD** today.
> 啊，会后我去跟他们**跟进**。他们应该今天**下班前**能准备好。

**A (Alex):** Perfect. That's all from me.
> 完美。我这边就这些。

**B (Maria):** Thanks, Alex. Alright, moving on — my update. We're **ahead of plan** on the Q2 roadmap. Two features got moved up from backlog...
> 谢谢 Alex。好，下一个——我的汇报。我们 Q2 路线图**进度超前**。两个功能从待办里提前提上来了……

─────────────────────────
🎯【语块精讲】（8 个）
─────────────────────────

**1. on track — 进度正常，按计划进行**
- 工作场景：The project is **on track** for the March release.
- 日常场景：I'm **on track** to finish reading this book by the weekend.

**2. behind schedule — 落后于进度**
- 工作场景：We're two weeks **behind schedule** on the migration.
- 日常场景：My spring cleaning is **behind schedule** this year.

**3. ahead of plan — 进度超前**
- 工作场景：We're **ahead of plan** on the hiring timeline.
- 日常场景：I finished my holiday shopping **ahead of plan** this year!

**4. pulled into — 被拉去/被卷入（某件事）**
- 工作场景：I got **pulled into** a meeting that wasn't even relevant to me.
- 日常场景：Sorry I'm late, I got **pulled into** helping my neighbor move a sofa.

**5. wrapped up — 完成了，收尾了**
- 工作场景：Let's make sure the report is **wrapped up** before the long weekend.
- 日常场景：I **wrapped up** the last episode of that series last night.

**6. circle back — 稍后再回来讨论/确认**
- 工作场景：Let's **circle back** to this topic after we get more data.
- 日常场景：Let me **circle back** with you on dinner plans after I check my schedule.

**7. blocked on — 被……卡住了**
- 工作场景：I'm **blocked on** the design review — can't move forward without it.
- 日常场景：I'm **blocked on** packing because I can't find my suitcase.

**8. EOD (End of Day) — 今天下班前**
- 工作场景：I'll send you the feedback by **EOD**.
- 日常场景：I promise to finish the dishes by **EOD**... or maybe tomorrow.

🔄【变造挑战】（3 题）
─────────────────────────

**1.** 你在周会上汇报项目进度，整体正常，但有个小问题需要同步。
> 💡 提示：用 on track + behind schedule 描述不同部分的进度。

**2.** 你被一个紧急 bug 拉走了，导致原定任务延期。你需要向同事解释情况。
> 💡 提示：用 pulled into + wrapped up + circle back 组织你的解释。

**3.** 你在等另一个团队的交付物，一直没收到，需要催一下。
> 💡 提示：用 blocked on + follow up + EOD 表达你的请求。

─────────────────────────

💡 **今日一句：**"We're **on track** with the sprint goal." — 下次周会，试着用 on track / behind schedule / ahead of plan 替代笼统的"doing fine"或"still working on it"。精确描述进度，是专业感的体现。
